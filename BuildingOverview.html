Building

The platoon building ontology defines the building vocabulary for the Platoon project. The platoon building ontology(plt:BuildingOntology) is an extension of seas Building ontology (seas:BuildingOntology). It reuses also some classes from schema ontology (sch:City, sch:Place, sch:Country). In addition of defined buildings on seas, some kind of buildings have been created; plt:DataCenter, plt:LogisticCenter, plt:Retail and plt:TransformerCenter are declared as subclasses of  plt:NonResidentialBuilding( which is equivalent of  seas:NonresidentialBuilding ), and plt:Canteen as a subclass of seas:BuildingSpace. The class plt:ClimateZone implements climate zone concept, it consists of areas with distinct climates can be classified using different climatic parameters. Climate zone can contain place (represented by the relation sch:ContainsPlace ), it can be also contained in Place (sch:ContainedInPlace). The concept open air zone(plt:OutdoorZone) and under cover zone(plt:IndoorZone) are a subclass of zone(seas:Zone). The relation energetic performance class (plt:energeticPerformanceClass) is defined to link building space to its the energy performance label(plt:EnergyPerformanceLabel).

Use cases:

  • Optimization of the control of the HVAC regarding occupancy (platoon pilot 3b). 

  • Optimization the local renewable energy resources (RES) and HVAC operation as function of building and RES characteristics, building comfort constraints, ambient conditions and energy market price following a multi-objective pattern which targets to reduce the overall energy bill and maximize the usage of RES(platoon pilot 3a). 

  • Building Heating & Cooling consumption Analysis and Forecast 

  • Balancing Regional Level 

  • Advanced energy management system for Tertiary Buildings
