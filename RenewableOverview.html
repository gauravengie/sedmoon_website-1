Wind Turbine

Pilot #1 concerns the wind turbine domain. It focusses on predictive maintenance in wind energy. The main goal of this pilot use case is to achieve early detection of faults in the electrical components of the powertrain: generator and converter. 

Through reasoning based on semantics the fault will be diagnosed based on these anomalies and potential root cause pinpointed out. Particularly for the latter a good semantic representation is required, since analytics-driven reasoning should be done on the data. 

In this pilot, focus is on offshore and onshore wind turbines equipped with a doubly fed induction generator.

Then the windTurbine ontology in Platoon project, aims to meet the needs identified in the pilot #1A by analyzing three heterogenous sources:

  • Detailed description given by the use case according to IEC-62559 template
  • SCADA data
  • Maintenance, anomalies, failure Modes, Code Alarms
  • Business expert and stakeholder interviews 

The figure below shows an extract of the diagram to represent the wind turbine ontology.

Mainly, Ontowind ontology is used and extended or mapped to complete the missing concepts meeting the pilot needs.
